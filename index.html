Future of Healthcare: Precision Medicine & Medical Intelligence

From generalized treatments to individualized solutions; from siloed health systems to integrated ecosystems; from reactive to preventive — medical intelligence and precision medicine heralds a powerful transformation in healthcare.

Harnessing ML for Pancreatic Cancer Detection

Employing urinary biomarkers, Plasma CA19-9, and demographic information to detect PDAC, PDAC resection-eligible cases, and chronic pancreatitis patients at risk of developing PDAC.

Eye Disease Diagnostics & Computer Vision

Using computer vision techniques with a ResNet-18 deep learning model for the detection of glaucoma, cataract, and diabetic retinopathy.

Healthcare Diagnostics Bot

Implementation of NLP techniques to power a rudimentary "GenAI bot." Input symptoms and get diagnostic and treatment outputs. (For research/fun only — not medical advice!)

Pancake the Pancreatic Cancer Navigator

PanCAI (Pancake) — an AI agent navigating patients and families through pancreatic cancer diagnosis, prognosis, treatment, and survival.

Colorectal Cancer Analytics

Clinical analytics project on colorectal cancer remission and recurrence data. Focused on disease-free survival longevity and therapy usage.

Health Equity & Integrated Health Systems: Rural Healthcare

Exploring integrated health systems in rural communities with an emphasis on data-driven technology and intelligent systems to ensure health equity and collaborative care.
